Tip of the day...


Navigate faster using Wikipedia shortcuts

Many of the pages in the Wikipedia namespace have specialized redirects called "shortcuts" that can be entered into the search box on the left side of your screen. Normally these shortcuts start with WP: and then a capitalized abbreviation for the page name. Some common shortcuts are: WP:DEPT, WP:FAQ, WP:FUN, WP:IMAGE, WP:VP, and of course, WP:TIP. A shortcut to the list of shortcuts is WP:CUT.

Read more: Wikipedia:List of shortcuts
