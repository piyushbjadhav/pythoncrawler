Featured content in Wikipedia

Featured content represents the best that Wikipedia can offer. This includes the articles, pictures, and other contributions that showcase the polished result of the collaborative efforts that drive Wikipedia. All featured content undergoes a thorough review process to ensure that it meets the highest standards, and can serve as the best example of our end goals. A small bronze star () in the top right corner of a page indicates that the content is featured. This page gives links to all of Wikipedia's featured content.

Featured articles

An emu

Eight featured articles were promoted this week.

  • Battle of Agua Dulce (nominated by Karanacs) The Battle of Agua Dulce was a skirmish between Mexican troops and Texians in 1836. The Texians were ambushed.
  • Emu (nominated by Cwmhiraeth) The emu is a large flightless Australian bird. It can be over six feet tall, almost as tall as an ostrich. They have very strong legs which they use to avoid attacks by dingoes.
  • Gateway Protection Programme (nominated by Cordless Larry) The Gateway Protection Programme is a British government scheme to resettle a number of refugees in the UK.
  • Kalidas (film) (nominated by Kailash29792) Kalidas is the first Indian film to have a soundtrack. It was made in 1931.
  • Mayfly (nominated by Cwmhiraeth) There are 3,000 species of mayflies. They are aquatic insects.
  • Menkauhor Kaiu (nominated by Iry-Hor) Menkauhor Kaiu was an Ancient Egyptian pharaoh, who reigned for eight years.
  • Moonraker (novel) (nominated by SchroCat) Moonraker is the third James Bond novel.
  • Thunderbirds (TV series) (nominated by SuperMarioMan) Thunderbirds was a childrens' TV series about International Rescue.
