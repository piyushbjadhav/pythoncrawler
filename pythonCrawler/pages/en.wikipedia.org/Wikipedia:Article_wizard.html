Welcome to the Wikipedia Article Wizard!

This wizard will help you through the process of submitting a new article to Wikipedia. If you have questions at any point, you can get help from other Wikipedians by going to the Wikipedia Teahouse or the help desk, or by clicking the Live Help Chat in the top right. Remember that Wikipedians are mostly volunteers, so be sure to look at available written guidance before requesting help. You can also play The Wikipedia Adventure and learn how to edit in an hour, or go through this brief tutorial.

Are you ready to create an article?

 Try editing a few existing articles before you create your first article. Unreferenced articles are often deleted. Learning how to cite your sources first increases your chances of success.

To learn more about the basics of editing, see this tutorial.

Before you get started, you should become familiar with some basic elements of editing, which will help you avoid some common mistakes.
