Weekdays, 11am ET

The most interesting news stories of the day. If you're talking about it, so are we, with anchors John Berman and Kate Bolduan.

Show Description

The PostTape: CNN anchor Anderson Cooper on the spot

Kate Bolduan and fellow anchor Anderson Cooper get personal about Big Macs, tattoos, and first kisses...

The PostTape: CNN anchor Don Lemon on the spot

Kate Bolduan and Don Lemon get real on bad fashion trends, home cooking, and the essential "Don Lemonness."

The PostTape: CNN anchor Richard Quest on the spot

Kate Bolduan and fellow anchor Richard Quest talk everything from airplanes to infomercials in a round of Table Topics.

Ex-inmate: Prison worker "showered inmate with gifts"

New details from former fellow inmate, Erik Jensen, on the relationship between prison employee Joyce Mitchell and escapee David Sweat.

Ex-inmate: Prison worker and escapee had sex

Erik Jensen, a former fellow inmate of the escaped prisoners in New York, describes his observations of the interactions between prison worker Joyce Mitchell and the escapees.

Legendary news anchor Dan Rather reflects on Vietnam

CNN's John Berman and Kate Bolduan sit down with legendary news anchor Dan Rather to talk about public opinion of television's first war.

CNN anchor gets put on the spot

Kate Bolduan turns the questions on co-anchor John Berman in a round of Table Topics.

Silicon Valley struggles with diversity

Randi Zuckerberg and Star Jones are trying to change the look of Silicon Valley. They speak with Kate Bolduan on ways to fix their diversity problem.

John Berman: I'm a Patriots fan -- shame on me

By John Berman, CNN
Shame on me.

Palestinian man stabs 9 on Israeli bus

A Palestinian man stabbed multiple passengers on a Tel Aviv bus in what Israeli police said was a terror attack. CNN's Elise Labott has more.

Boston doctor fatally shot in hospital

Officials say a doctor was shot and the suspect died of a self-inflicted gunshot wound at Brigham and Women's Hospital in Boston. CNN affiliate WCVB has more.

Several Disneyland employees diagnosed with measles

The Orange County Health Care Agency has confirmed that "several" Disneyland employees have been diagnosed with measles. CNN Affiliate KCAL/KCBS has more.

State of the Union: 68 years in 68 seconds

A mash-up of State of the Union addresses from Truman in 1947 to Obama in 2015. We cover 68 years in 68 seconds.
