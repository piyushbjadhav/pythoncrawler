    Previous CNN Films 

    • Life Itself

      When Roger Ebert died in 2013 after battling cancer, he was arguably the world's most famous film critic. "Life Itself" is a revealing documentary based on Ebert's memoir.

    • Dinosaur 13

      CNN Films' "Dinosaur 13" documents the discovery of the most complete Tyrannosaurus Rex fossil ever found -- and the incredible legal battle to determine who owned it.

    • Ivory Tower

      "Ivory Tower" questions the cost, value and methods of America's higher education system. The documentary explores a potential crisis that could touch an entire generation of young Americans.

    • Whitey: US v. James J. Bulger

      "Whitey: US v. James J. Bulger" details the capture and conviction of the notorious Boston gangster James "Whitey" Bulger.

    • Documented

      Explore the journey of undocumented immigrant and Pulitzer Prize-winning journalist Jose Antonio Vargas.

    • Sole Survivor

      Only a handful of people have come through major plane crashes alive and alone. CNN Films' "Sole Survivors" examines four of those stories: The toddler, the "Miracle Girl," the co-pilot and the seeker. They survived when no one else did.

    • An Unreal Dream

      Michael Morton, who spent 25 years in prison after he was wrongly convicted in his wife's murder, says he forgives a prosecutor who failed to share evidence in his trial.

    • Blackfish

      Blackfish traces a 39-year history of killer whales in captivity leading up to the 2010 killing of Sea World trainer Dawn Brancheau by the 12,000-pound orca, Tilikum, a whale previously associated with the death of two other people.

    • Pandora's Promise

      Through the intensely personal stories of environmental thinkers, "Pandora's Promise" reveals a rift within the environmental movement as they describe their individual journeys of "defection," in the debate over how to manage climate change.
