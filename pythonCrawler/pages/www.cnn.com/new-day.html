6-9am ET

Chris Cuomo, Alisyn Camerota & Michaela Pereira report the latest news and top stories. Victor Blackwell & Christi Paul host weekends.

Show Description

Hillary Clinton bouncing back in new poll

Hillary Clinton is taking a stronger lead in the Democratic presidential primary race. If Vice President Joe Biden decides not to run, a new poll shows her numbers would climb even higher.

Carson spokesman on Muslim remark: 'He won't apologize'

CNN's Alisyn Camerota speaks with Ben Carson's spokesman and Conservative radio Host Armstrong Williams about Carson's controversial Muslim remarks.

Fiery RV sets other vehicles, power lines ablaze

A Pennsylvania woman was trapped inside a rolling RV that was engulfed in flames, setting power lines and other vehicles on fire.

After summer of Trump, Producer Emilio Estefan, celebrities combat rhetoric aimed at Latinos

By Jeremy Diamond, CNN
More than two dozen Latino musicians and celebrities joined forces with Grammy-winning producer Emilio Estefan to combat what he called "negativity about Latinos" in the current political discourse with a video entitled "We Are All Mexican."

Rep. Black defends position against Planned Parenthood

Rep. Diane Black, (R) Tennessee, defends her position against funding Planned Parenthood to CNN's Chris Cuomo.

Irving police chief speaks on teen clock arrest

Irving Police Chief Larry Boyd speaks on the decisions that led to the arrest 14-year-old Ahmed Mohamed, who brought a homemade clock to school.

Meet the woman who lived the story in 'Captive'

Actor David Oyelowo and Ashley Smith talk about the story behind the movie "Captive."

Trump doesn't correct man who calls Obama a Muslim

At a campaign rally, Republican frontrunner Donald Trump does not correct one of his supporters when he calls Obama a Muslim.

Fiorina: It's still different for women

Republican presidential candidate Carly Fiorina talks about her debate performance and how she responded to Donald Trump's quote about "her face."

What's trending from the #GOPDebate?

CNN's Michaela Pereira breaks down the top moments and themes from social media around the GOP debate.

Gov. Chris Christie responds to fact checkers

Gov. Chris Christie responds to those questioning a statement he made about the timeline of his nomination and appointment as a U.S. attorney.

Bernie Sanders: GOP debate was 'really painful'

Democratic presidential candidate Bernie Sanders shared his thoughts on the Republican debate with CNN's New Day. He also addressed immigration reform and Bobby's Jindal's jab at socialism.

What should candidates do at the #CNNDebate?

CNN Political Commentator Errol Louis and CNN Political Analyst John Avlon discuss tips for the candidates before the #CNNDebate.

Police: Delta State shooting suspect shoots, kills self

Delta State University shooting suspect Shannon Lamb shot and killed himself late Monday night, police said. CNN's Alina Machado reports.

Kim Davis: I am no hero

Kentucky county clerk Kim Davis speaks out on her first day back at work.

California wildfire victim: We lost everything

Thousands in northern California fled for their lives as brutal wildfires gobbled up their homes by the hundreds. CNN's Dan Simon reports from Middletown, California.

Men accuse cop in Blake incident of prior brutality

There are other accusations of police brutality against officer James Frascatore.
