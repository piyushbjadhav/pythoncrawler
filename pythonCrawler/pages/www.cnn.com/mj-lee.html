About 

    MJ Lee is a reporter for CNN Politics and CNNMoney based in New York, covering the intersection of politics and finance. She writes about economic and financial themes that collide with the world of politics, including storylines emerging from the 2016 campaign.

    Prior to joining CNN, MJ was a finance and politics reporter at Politico in Washington, D.C. Working out of Capitol Hill, she covered Congress, the Federal Reserve, financial regulations, economic data and the banking community.

    MJ was born in South Korea and grew up in Hong Kong. She has a bachelor's degree in government and Chinese from Georgetown University.
