    By Julian Zelizer

Early on he launched a series of ad hominem attacks, like telling Rand Paul he shouldn't even be on the stage and making quips about his looks. This has been his strategy since entering the race -- constantly attack and constantly be attacked.

This stuff doesn't hold up to scrutiny. The question is whether any is ever applied to Trump.
