Schedule of Dates for the Match

For 2016-2017 postdoctoral positions, the Match will be conducted in two phases:

Phase I - ORTHO and/or ANES Programs Only:

A first match will be carried out for ORTHO and ANES positions only. All ORTHO and ANES programs, and any applicants interested in matching to an ORTHO or ANES position, must submit Rank Order Lists by November 20, 2015, for Phase I of the Match.

Applicants who obtain an ORTHO or ANES position in Phase I of the Match will not be eligible to participate in Phase II of the Match. Any applicant who did not submit a Rank Order List for Phase I, or who submitted a Rank Order List but was not matched in Phase I, may submit a Rank Order List for Phase II of the Match.

Phase II - GPR, AEGD, OMS and/or PED Programs Only:

Any applicant interested in matching to a GPR, AEGD, OMS or PED position, and who did not match to a position in Phase I, must submit a Rank Order List by January 8, 2016. All GPR, AEGD, OMS and PED programs must also submit their Rank Order Lists by the same date.

 

Complete Schedule of Dates

All participants must adhere to the complete Schedule of Dates below:

APRIL - MAY, 2015

Institution Agreements for participation in the Match are sent to program directors. Program directors must return their Institution Agreements to National Matching Services Inc.

MAY 20, 2015

Beginning on this date applicants can register for the Match. Prior to registering, applicants should review information on the Match web site at natmatch.com/dentres. Applicants register for the Match via the online registration portal shared by the Match and the ADEA Postdoctoral Application Support Service (PASS). The registration portal can be accessed from the Match web site, or directly at portal.passweb.org.

Applicants must apply to programs independently of the Match. Application deadlines for programs vary; therefore, applicants should check with programs regarding their deadline dates.

AUGUST 1, 2015

By this date, a preliminary list of programs participating in the Match will be available on the Match web site at natmatch.com/dentres.

OCTOBER 1, 2015

Recommended date by which applicants should register for the Match.

OCTOBER 23, 2015

By this date, instructions for submitting Rank Order Lists and obtaining Match results will be available to applicants and program directors registered to participate in the Match.

NOVEMBER 2, 2015

The online Match system will open on this date for Match participants to submit Rank Order Lists for Phase I of the Match for ORTHO and ANES positions.

NOVEMBER 20, 2015

Final date for submission of applicant and program Rank Order Lists for Phase I of the Match for ORTHO and ANES positions.

DECEMBER 2, 2015

Results of the Match for Phase I are released to applicants and program directors. No action to fill ORTHO or ANES positions remaining unfilled after Phase I is to be taken prior to 12 noon Eastern Time on this date.

DECEMBER 2, 2015 - JANUARY 1, 2016

Program directors of ORTHO and ANES programs must send letters of confirmation of the Match result for Phase I to matched applicants within 10 days of the release of the Match results. Applicants must sign and return the letters of confirmation within 30 days of the release of the Match results.

DECEMBER 14, 2015

The online Match system will open on this date for Match participants to submit Rank Order Lists for Phase II of the Match for GPR, AEGD, OMS and PED positions.

JANUARY 8, 2016

Final date for submission of applicant and program Rank Order Lists for Phase II of the Match for GPR, AEGD, OMS and PED positions.

JANUARY 25, 2016

Results of the Match for Phase II are released to applicants and program directors. No action to fill positions remaining unfilled after Phase II is to be taken prior to 12 noon Eastern Time on this date.

JANUARY 25, 2016 - FEBRUARY 24, 2016

Program directors of GPR, AEGD, OMS and PED programs must send letters of confirmation of the Match result for Phase II to matched applicants within 10 days of the release of the Match results. Applicants must sign and return the letters of confirmation within 30 days of the release of the Match results.
