Nuclear Medicine Technologist

In nuclear medicine, radionuclides—unstable atoms that emit radiation spontaneously—are used to diagnose and treat disease.

Average Salary: $65,000 - $0

Years in school: 1 - 0 (after high school graduation)

Job outlook: Excellent
