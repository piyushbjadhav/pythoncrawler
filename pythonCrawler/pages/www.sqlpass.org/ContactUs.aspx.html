Day-to-day operations at PASS, including tactical projects and major events, are overseen and run by a management team contracted by the Board of Directors. Since 2007, PASS has been under the management of Christianson & Company, based in Vancouver, Canada. In that time, PASS has witnessed phenomenal growth. The management team – led by Judy Christianson, principal of Christianson & Company – are dedicated to enriching and empowering the Microsoft data community, particularly PASS's members and volunteers, so that PASS can succeed in its mission to help the community Connect, Share, and Learn.














   
   
JUDY CHRISTIANSON

Executive Manager & Principal

Judy is the principal ofChristianson & Company, the management team responsible for the day-to-day operations and management of PASS. Christianson & Company has been in business for over 20 years, working on major corporate events for clients such as Electronic Arts, Kodak Canada, the Kodak Graphic Communications Group, and Creo Products Inc., to mention a few. In addition, Judy and the C&C team have produced a number of successful celebrity fundraisers for charitable organizations, including the Steve Nash Foundation, Mark Messier in support of Canuck Place, and the Courtnall Celebrity Classic.

Judy brings over a decade of association management experience to the organization and has served as Director on a variety of boards, as Executive Director to the Creo and Kodak Graphic Users Association boards, and most recently, as Executive Manager for PASS. Judy’s main focus is providing the overall vision and leadership for PASS while ensuring all resources are in place to support the needs of the association, the Board of Directors, and the 130,000+ members of PASS.

Contact Judy
