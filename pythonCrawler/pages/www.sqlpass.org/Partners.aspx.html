PASS Global Alliance Partners

PASS Global Alliance Program Partners are a vital part of the community, supporting the organization’s many training and networking opportunities, including free learning events. Thanks to the following Partners, whose comprehensive, strategic relationship with PASS helps provide a strong foundation for the worldwide data community year-round.

Dell Software

With data growth and complexity driving the need for better management of leading databases, SQL Server plays a critical role in the IT management ecosystem. That’s why Dell Software continues to focus on providing solutions for SQL Server that assist organizations worldwide in solving business critical challenges, and why we are committed to remaining a leader now and well into the future. Monitor, manage, protect and improve your SQL Server environment with solutions from Dell.

   

HP

At HP we don’t just believe in the power of technology. We believe in the power of people when technology works for them. To help you create. To make the digital tangible. To harness the power of human information. At HP we work to make what you do matter even more. More information about HP (NYSE: HPQ) is available at http://www.hp.com

Hortonworks

Hortonworks develops, distributes and supports the only 100% open source distribution of Apache Hadoop explicitly architected, built and tested for enterprise grade deployments. It is the only Hadoop-based platform available on both Linux and Windows.
   

SQL Sentry

SQL Sentry delivers software that optimizes the performance of Microsoft SQL Server, Analysis Services, SharePoint, and Windows environments.

Idera

Idera is a leading provider of IT performance monitoring solutions. Our products help database administrators, system admins, application owners and network engineers improve the health, availability and performance of their IT systems. More than 13,000 customers around the world – including many Fortune 1,000 enterprises, government agencies, and some of the world’s largest datacenters and cloud infrastructure providers – rely on Idera products to keep their systems optimized and IT operations running smoothly.

Kroll Ontrack

Providing data recovery services since 1985, Ontrack® PowerControls™ from Kroll Ontrack granularly restores dropped SQL tables, saving DBA’s hours of time versus full database restores. This tool supports snapshots or native SQL backups, lets you preview table content before restoration and drag and drop copied tables directly to your desired environment. Kroll Ontrack also offers industry-leading data recovery solutions and supports data professionals with learning events and a rich resources library.

Pyramid Analytics

Pyramid Analytics offers a turnkey enterprise analytics platform that makes it easy to self-serve and share trusted BI content. It complements Microsoft SQL Server Analysis Services and leverages all of the features of the Microsoft BI stack.

Become a Partner

The PASS Global Alliance Program was created with the mission of building new business opportunities and long-term partnerships with companies who have supported the PASS Community over the years. This exclusive program gives companies the unique opportunity to collaborate with PASS partner, marketing, and leadership teams, helping to maximize their return on investment. Our program offers three membership tiers: Platinum, Gold, and Silver - each providing unique and exclusive benefits available only to Partners. Interested in becoming a PASS Partner?
