Chelsea star Eden Hazard is back to his best, Nemanja Matic warns rivals

NEMANJA MATIC has warned Chelsea’s rivals that Eden Hazard will now get back to his best after scoring his first goal of the season.

By Luke Gardener / Published 21st September 2015

Hazard, who had been struggling in this campaign, scored with a deflected effort late on to secure a 2-0 win against Arsenal on Saturday.

It was the Belgium international’s 50th goal for the club but Matic believes it will have much more significance for Chelsea’s and Hazard’s prospects this term.

He told the London Evening Standard: "It’s a big goal for Eden.

"He needs to score goals for his confidence and to be on his top level. He is very important for our team. I am very happy he scored his first goal and I hope it gives him more self-belief.

"He needs to know that he is the best player in the Premier League and use his qualities. When he scored it was an important moment in the season for him."

Matic believes the victories against Arsenal and Maccabi Tel Aviv, in the Champions League last week, will be regarded as the turning point of Chelsea’s campaign.

"In two games we scored six goals and didn’t concede," he said. "That’s very important for confidence and for team spirit."
