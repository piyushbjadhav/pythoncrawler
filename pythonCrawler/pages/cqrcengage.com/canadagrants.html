Canadian Grant Funding

The Canadian Institutes for Health Research provides federal funding for Canadian health research. Grant opportunities may be searched through Research Net.

 

Some links of interest:

  • Overview of CIHR funding
  • News and recent developments in health research funding
  • CIHR grant application process
  • CIHR funding policies
  • Database of funding decisions

 

Depending on your research interests, additional funding opportunities may be available through the Natural Sciences and Engineering Research Council (NSERC) or the Social Sciences and Humanities Research Council (SSH).
