        Pros:

        -Great ambiance
        -Big place, even with so many people it doesn't feel crowded. 5 stars
        - Food is great. Nothing super fancy but very decent 4 stars
        - Entertainment is excellent. They play all type of music for everyone's taste. 5 stars
        - Price is decent for banquet menu's 5 stars
        - Service is what I really loved. I am always very observant about all the little things, but the bus boys were always super attentive, always noticing if you would like to try a new salad, etc. Always changed dirty plates and made sure everyone has alcohol in their glasses. This is where they really stood out from the rest of Russian restaurants. 5 stars
        - BYOB like most Russian restaurants

        Cons:

        - The dance floor is a bit too small. Gets a bit too crowded.

        We celebrated a friend's birthday and had a blast. Would definitely come again. Out of all Russian banquet restaurants, if you care about service, ambiance and entertainment, this is a place to go to.

        I'm surprised that I haven't written a review about this place already
        This place tries really hard to look like a club, but in reality it is just a restaurant with a dance floor and live music. The singers are very good, singing in Russian, English and Spanish too. They take requests for any songs you'd like them to sing so that's a plus.
        The dance floor is a decent size so you wont feel like you're getting squished
        The service was EXCELLENT, the waiters really do care about their patrons, always checking up asking if I needed anything, always making sure my glass Is full and they serve the food for you , you get what you pay for. when I went with my two girlfriends I paid about 65 a person without tip, but its a three course meal of appetizers, entrée and dessert so its fair. This place is a BYOB place, typical for a Russian establishment.
        I admit when I first saw the bouncers in the front I was a  bit intimidated, they were big dudes, but they really do care about the safety of the people, they were helping open drunk girl call a cab and made sure she got home safe. The décor itself is beautiful I think that's what makes me love this place the most, from the lighting to the table setup to the cleanliness its what every restaurant should embody.
        The food was good, the filet mignon was on par, the mashed potatoes were creamy garlicy and seasoned well, the green salads were perfect portions plus the endless amount of fresh bread and butter which im a sucker for lol , large enough to be shared with my friends. The desserts weren't bad, cute little cakes and tea or coffee to go along with it, and fruits so I my stomach didn't feel like it was about to explode.
        Would I come back here? Yes! I just wish they had a crowd that wasn't primarily Russian, they should reach out to other members of the community

        Best steak in NYC
        Yes, Club Cats is expensive. Quality and excellence cost money. I have been to other places......they do not compare! Club Cats knows how to prepare steak and provide a memorable experience. Although crowded, once you are seated, you are no longer aware of others. Our waiter has always treated us like we are his only customers. 1 jumbo shrimp cocktail, 3 slices bacon, 2 green salads, 2 sliced tomatoes and onions, 1 creamed spinach and porterhouse for 3, medium, sliced and served in its juices with 1 bottle of Chatenauf de Pape and 1 Brooklyn Lager is our typical order, followed by 3 coffees with amaretto and 1 slice of pecan pie with schlag! Yes, expensive, but the most delicious steak and sides ever. A memorable meal we all reminisce and talk about until we return. Not sure about prices of each item, but we usually pay about $80 per person, because we have wine and beer, plus tip. Always generously tip our waiter who does a wonderful service for us. The waiters always make you feel that you are their only table. Friendly and attentive. We have always lingered over coffee and have never been rushed out, no matter how crowded the restaurant is. If you want steak, Club Cats is your best choice.
