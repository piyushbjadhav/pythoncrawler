        I have never been to Cats Café prior to this visit despite having had lived in the area from 1996 to 2009.  We were invited for an event and here is the breakdown:
        1. Ambiance-5*-very cozy space, designed to accommodate no more than 100 people with a large dance floor.  Comfortable chairs and plenty of room to spread out.
        2. Service-5*-Sergey was the captain for our table, he is a friendly young gentleman who was always there, making sure our plates and glasses were filled, he truly made the evening flow very smoothly.
        3. Music-4*-It seemed that they sang most of the songs live as opposed to lip-synching that has become quite popular in Russian restaurants, but at times, the music was just way too loud for such a small place.  Otherwise, they kept the party going.
        4. Food-3*-while the food was good, it was not great, the way I judge restaurant food this day is "Can I reproduce this at home?" and maybe 20 years ago when consumer options were limited to crap from mediocre supermarkets, I would have been wowed, however, most of the stuff that was served I could make at home very easily.  Of course, for someone who has no idea what burrata is or how a good skirt steak tastes, this food might taste amazing, perhaps, I have just become spoiled.

        Final verdict-this is a good place for a family party but I would not come here expecting to be knocked off my feet.

        Specialties

        Brand New French/New American Restaurant with inventive cuisine by renowned chef, live entertainment, attentive staff and amazing atmosphere. Open 7 days a week from lunch till late night. Large parties welcome. Outdoor dining. Full bar. Weekend brunches. Lunch specials.

        History

        Established in 2013.

        Cats on the Bay was opened in 2013 in the same place where the original Cats Cafe was destroyed during Hurricane Sandy. Our mission is to provide inventive cuisine in a fun and entertaining environment. With over a year of success, we look forward to adding you to our long history of happy customers.

        Meet the Business Owner

        Peysakh N.
        Business Owner

        Peysakh has been in the restaurant industry for over 10 years. Peysakh enjoys being able to serve the public at Cats on the Bay, where our innovative blend of French, American and Russian cuisine is the driving force of the business. Peysakh hopes to provide you such a positive experience you'll remember to return to Cats on the Bay when you're in need of a great meal.
