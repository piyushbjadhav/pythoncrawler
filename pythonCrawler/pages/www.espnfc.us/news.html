Chelsea duo Gary Cahill, Diego Costa suffer pre-Arsenal injury scares

Gary Cahill and Diego Costa both suffered injury scares that marred Chelsea's penalty win over Barcelona in Washington D.C. on Tuesday.

Blues manager Jose Mourinho confirmed after the match that both were receiving treatment, but appeared unconcerned with the severity of either injury as Sunday's Community Shield clash with Arsenal looms.
